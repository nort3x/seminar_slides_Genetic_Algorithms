### Minimize:
$\text{MSE}(\vec{N},\vec{\lambda}) = \dfrac{1}{N} \sum\limits_{data} (c_i - \~c(ti))^2$

#### with:

<p>

$data = \{(t_1,c_1), (t_2,c_2),..., (t_N,c_N)\}$
</p>
<p>

$\~c(t)= N_0(1-e^{-\lambda_0 t}) + N_1(1-e^{-\lambda_1 t}) + \text{background}(t)$
</p>
